Passwords are still common, but **passkeys** are changing the default.

## Practical advice
- Use a password manager for legacy accounts
- Prefer passkeys when available
- Keep recovery options strong and up to date

## Migration plan
1. Enable MFA
2. Add a passkey
3. Remove weak recovery methods
